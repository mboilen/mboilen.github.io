24-hour time (default is 12 hour)
Enable color
When color is enabled, Seattle Bus will use yellow to indicate a long delay until the next bus, and red indicates the last bus of the night. When color is disabled, then it will use "!" to indicate a long delay and "!!" to indicate the last bus of the night. This is disabled on Pebble watches with black and white displays.
